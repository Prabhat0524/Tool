How It Works

1. Upload Your Image

Drag and drop your image file into the upload area or click to browse your files. We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats with a maximum file size of 10MB.

2. Adjust Settings

Choose your desired compression level and output format. The quality slider lets you balance between file size and image quality.

3. Download Result

After compression, you'll see a side-by-side comparison with file size information. Download your optimized image with one click.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service really free?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges. We may display ads to support the service.

What image formats do you support?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can keep the original format or convert to any of these formats during compression.

Is there a file size limit?

Currently, we accept images up to 10MB in size. For larger files, we recommend using professional desktop software.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all processing happens in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.

What's the best format for web use?

WebP generally provides the best compression with good quality. For maximum compatibility, JPEG is still the safest choice.
